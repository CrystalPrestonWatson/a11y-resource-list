## Books

Books that cover accessibility, inclusive design, disability studies, and practical implementation:

- [**Accessibility for Everyone** — Laura Kalbag](https://abookapart.com/products/accessibility-for-everyone)
- [**Mismatch: How Inclusion Shapes Design** — Kat Holmes](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262539487/mismatch/)
- [**A Web for Everyone: Designing Accessible User Experiences** — Sarah Horton and Whitney Quesenbery](https://rosenfeldmedia.com/books/a-web-for-everyone/)
- [**Inclusive Design for a Digital World: Designing with Accessibility in Mind** — Regine Gilbert](https://reginegilbert.com/inclusive-design-for-a-digital-world)
- [**Accessible Social: A Beginner's Guide to Creating Inclusive Social Media Content** — Alexa Heinrich](https://www.accessible-social.com/)
- [**What Every Engineer Should Know About Digital Accessibility** — David Sloan and Sarah Horton](https://www.routledge.com/What-Every-Engineer-Should-Know-About-Digital-Accessibility/Sloan-Horton/p/book/9781032040242)
- [**Structured Negotiation: A Winning Alternative to Lawsuits** — Lainey Feingold](https://www.lflegal.com/book/)
- [**Android Accessibility by Tutorials** — Victoria Gonda](https://www.kodeco.com/books/android-accessibility-by-tutorials)
- [**Demystifying Disability: What to Know, What to Say, and How to Be an Ally** — Emily Ladau](https://emilyladau.com/demystifying-disability/)
- [**The Architecture of Disability: Buildings, Cities, and Landscapes Beyond Access** — David Gissen](https://www.upress.umn.edu/book-division/books/the-architecture-of-disability)
- [**Black Disability Politics** — Sami Schalk](https://samischalk.com/black-disability-politics/)
- [**Being Heumann: An Unrepentant Memoir of a Disability Rights Activist** — Judith Heumann](https://judithheumann.com/being-heumann/)
- [**Disability Visibility: First Person Stories from the 21st Century** — Ed. Alice Wong](https://disabilityvisibilityproject.com/book/)
- [**The Accessibility Operations Guidebook** — Devon Persing](https://devonpersing.netlify.app/a11y-ops-guidebook/)
- [**Against Technoableism: Rethinking Who Needs Improvement** — Ashley Shew](https://www.akpress.org/against-technoableism.html)
- [**The Inclusive Speaker** — Denis Boudreau](https://inklusiv.ca/the-inclusive-speaker/)
